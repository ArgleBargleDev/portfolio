"So You're Back From Outer Space"

As part of A Hat in Time's Death Wish mode, I was tasked with creating a lot of remixes of various existing levels. "So You're Back From Outer Space" is a remix of the game's first jumping tutorial, where the scary alien Hat Kid chases the poor mafia around this scaffolding.

Since the original level was very short, I developed an all new segment that starts where the original level ends and circles around the island! This segment begins at the ramp leading to the cannon, shown in the video's level intro. Since this is one of the first Death Wish levels, I wanted to lure players into a false sense of security by keeping the original level mostly the same (except for a few additional enemies) before surprising them with the new content.

This level is one of the earliest Death Wish levels and is built to test a variety of skills that will be useful in the later levels.

Death Wish: Challenge Levels

The core concept of Death Wish was scary challenges that make the player sweat. Bombastic situations thought up by Snatcher, a trickster character who simply wants you dead. Basically, it's a difficult mode for players who thought A Hat in Time was too easy.

While I could have just added a few enemies and called it a day, Snatcher is a true sadist and a fan-favorite character, so I wanted to capture his personality in the mode. This meant the levels had to be flashy and panic-enducing, where the player would turn a corner and be faced with something really over-the-top and dramatic. This way, when the player overcomes these challenges, they get a more intense feeling of relief and triumph.

Death Wish Level Overview

A Hat in Time has very tight controls and a lot of mobility, so this mode was designed to test the upper limits of what the character is capable of. As such, it developed a dedicated cult following of people who sought to complete all 111 challenges. Even though it came out in 2018, the Death Wish challenge scene is still alive years later!
